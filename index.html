I am a quantum chemist with experience in quantum mechanics, linear algebra, scientific computing, and science communication.

I have recently defended my Ph.D. thesis at Rice University from the Scuseria group. My thesis explored the role of pairing in strongly correlated electronic structures. This work led to the development of quantum wavefunction methods tailored for paired fermionic and spin one-half many-body systems.
