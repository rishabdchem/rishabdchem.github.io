I am a quantum chemist with experience in quantum mechanics, linear algebra, scientific computing, and science communication.

I recently completed my Ph.D. at Rice University from the Scuseria group. My thesis focused on developing quantum wavefunction methods tailored for strongly correlated ground states of paired fermionic and spin-1/2 many-body systems. I have helped develop generalizations of the Dicke state and the elementary symmetric polynomial.
